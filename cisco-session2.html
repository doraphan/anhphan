CISCO MENTORME SESSION 2: Cisco Overview and A Day in the Life of a Systems Engineer


Highlights of the day: The presentations of Cori Drogemuller - Cisco Head of Communication and Nilram Azadpeyma - Systems Engineer.

The presentation about Cisco is remarkably inspiring which gives us insightful knowledge about Cisco. If we talk about Cisco, there are numerous milestones from its establishment in 1986 to its growth to be a globally robust networking organisation. So, I am going to select some facts that came as surprise to me:

  •    #1. Cisco was founded by married couple. Everything initially started in a small garage and what we see now is an internationally huge business that dominates the market of networking hardware, telecommunication and other technology and cloud service.

  •    #2. The meaning behind Cisco’s logo: This image demonstrates the revolution of Cisco logo. The original 1985 Cisco logo design was inspired by the famous landmark which symbolises San Francisco to the rest of the world’s knowledge. Just from looking at the current Cisco logo which is more suitable in the digital era, I thought blue lines altogether remind me of the unmistakable icon of Wifi network. Perhaps it makes sense since one of Cisco’s signature products is Wifi routers. Much to my surprise, what I knew is just a small part of its whole meaning. The Golden Gate bridge stands between San Francisco to Marin County, representing “connectivity”. With the same message, the bridge in Cisco design connects problems to their solutions. In a bigger perspective, it connects possibility, hope and passion.

  •    #3. Cisco gets involved in various social activities: for example: connecting the homeless, supporting youth right across New Zealand, saving rhinos and people lives, etc.
